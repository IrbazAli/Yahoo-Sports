The Bangladesh cricket team is touring Sri Lanka in June and July 2025 to play the Sri Lanka cricket team.[1] The tour consists of two Test, three One Day International (ODI) and three Twenty20 Internationals (T20I) matches.[2][3] The Test series forms part of the 2025–2027 ICC World Test Championship.[4] In May 2025, the Sri Lanka Cricket Board (SLC) announced the schedule of the series.[5]
